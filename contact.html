Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase artwork?

Simply browse our collection, add your favorite pieces to cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You'll see exact costs at checkout.

How can I become an Arteya artist?

We're always looking for talented artists! Please email your portfolio to artists@arteya.com with "Artist Application" in the subject line.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all artwork in its original condition. Custom and commissioned pieces may have different terms.
