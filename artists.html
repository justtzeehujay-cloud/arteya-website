Our Talented Artists

Discover the creative minds behind the beautiful artworks in our collection. Each artist brings a unique perspective and style.

Cindy Sherman

Photography & Conceptual Art

Renowned for her conceptual portraits that explore identity and social roles through self-transformation.

Jeff Koons

Sculpture & Pop Art

Known for his reproductions of banal objects and controversial explorations of consumer culture.

Takashi Murakami

Superflat Movement & Anime

Blending fine art with pop culture to create vibrant works that challenge traditional art boundaries.

Yayoi Kusama

Installation & Polka Dots

World-famous for her infinity rooms and obsessive use of polka dots in immersive installations.

Thomas Ruff

Photography & Digital Art

Pioneering digital manipulation in photography to explore perception and technological mediation.

Maria Lassnig

Body Awareness & Painting

Known for her "body awareness" paintings that explore physical sensations and self-perception.

Olivia Parker

Impressionism & Color Theory

Capturing fleeting moments of light and color with loose brushwork and a vibrant, expressive style.

Daniel Kim

Digital & Mixed Media

Pushing boundaries by combining traditional techniques with digital innovation and mixed media approaches.

Marina Abramović

Performance Art

Pioneer of performance art exploring the relationship between performer and audience, body and mind.

Thomas Wright

Sculpture & 3D Art

Transforming raw materials into expressive three-dimensional forms that explore space, volume, and texture.
